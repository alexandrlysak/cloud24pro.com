HACCP SYSTEM

HACCP or the Food Safety Management Quality System is, first of all, preventive actions, systematic identification, assessment and control of potential hazardous factors (biological, chemical, physical) that arise during the production process. Therefore, one of the characteristic features of the new economic policy is attention focused on the manufacture of safe food products at the level of world standards.

The HACCP system in the food industry aims to cover all aspects of the safety of products of each link in the food chain, from growing and harvesting, purchasing raw materials to the moment the consumer uses the food product.

ISO 9001 certification

The international standard ISO 9001 regulates the requirements for a quality management system in enterprises and organizations that contribute to the improvement of management processes, effective business management and increased competitiveness.

The DSTU ISO 9001 standard is universal, it can be used by enterprises or organizations of any form of ownership and areas that provide certain services, regardless of their size and scope of activity.

ISO 9001 certification Certification to ISO 9001 - a voluntary procedure. However, if you want to introduce a quality management system to guarantee the ability of your organization to provide high-quality manufacture of goods or provide services, then the implementation of ISO 9001 will be useful to you.

To obtain an ISO 9001 certificate, organizations need to show their ability to meet regulatory requirements, effectively apply the system and organize their work to meet customer needs.

ADVANTAGES OF CERTIFICATION OF QUALITY MANAGEMENT SYSTEM ACCORDING TO ISO 9001 FOLLOWING:

  • Improving business performance and company reliability status - A functioning quality system according to ISO 9001 allows you to organize the processes of delivery of goods and services that will satisfy the needs of customers, as well as legislative and regulatory requirements.
  • Benefits in tendering - Quite often, one of the requirements for tender proposals is the presence of an ISO 9001 certificate, confirming that the Manufacturer has an implemented management system that ensures the implementation of quality control of manufactured products or provided services.
