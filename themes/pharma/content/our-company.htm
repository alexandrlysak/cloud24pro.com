Our Profile

WE ARE CONTINUOUSLY WORKING ON INCREASING THE LEVEL OF OUR SERVICES.

We are an experienced company providing a full range of different types of product tests, materials required for certification and standardization to meet certain quality standards.

We are constantly working to increase the level of integration in research, the development of new methods for conducting product verification at the proper level. A team of experienced employees in various industries helps us with this.

We are working to ensure that the name of our company is synonymous with the quality of products that have been tested with our participation.

Also - we guarantee high quality service and professional partnership for product quality control. Our Partners and Customers have an extensive sales and distribution market in both the private and public sectors, with the expansion of the reach of the strategically selected Asian and European markets.

Growing experience in various fields of research helps us assist in a variety of processes for conducting product quality checks. The production facilities used by us and our Partners are equipped with modern equipment that ensures all the necessary research.

  • ISO 9001 2015 Certified and SAP-Powered Central Logistics

Our Vision and Mission

We strive to become the leading company in providing any services to support standardization and certification of products for the European and Asian markets.

Our goals are to improve people's health through influencing the products they use and consume. Thanks to our commitment to innovation and our commitment to serving the community in which we operate, we will strive to create sustainable values and high quality standards for product verification.

We are committed to shaping the future for quality standards for products tested with our participation.
